Cookies on Recruit registry volunteers

What are cookies?

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

They store information about how you use the website, such as the pages you visit.

Cookies are not viruses or computer programs. They are very small so do not take up much space.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • make our website work, for example by keeping it secure
  • recognise your device, so you do not need to enter the same information more than once
  • measure how many people are using the services we provide, so we can make them easier and faster to use
  • measure how you use our website, such as the links you click on

Cookies cannot be used to identify you personally. Find out more about cookies and how to manage them.
