Recruit Registry Volunteers

Setting up an account on Recruit Registry Volunteers will enable you to create a pre-screener to send to potential volunteers for your study

This is an overview of the platform:

  • While creating the pre-screener, you will be able to save it at any point and continue later.
  • Once submitted, your pre-screener will be sent to our admins for approval.

Once approved, a link to pre-screener will be sent to volunteers by email.

After your volunteers fill out the pre-screener and submit it, you will be able to see the results on this platform.
