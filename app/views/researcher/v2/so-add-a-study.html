Recruit participants to your study

Use the National Institute for Health and Care Research (NIHR) volunteer registry to find people interested in taking part in your health or care research study.

The service gives researchers access to a UK-wide pool of volunteers who have agreed to hear about research opportunities.

What you'll get

If your study is eligible, the NIHR team will:

  • identify volunteers who match your study requirements
  • contact suitable volunteers on your behalf
  • support you through the recruitment process
{{ insetText({ text: "Once you've registered, the NIHR team will contact you within 3 working days." }) }}

If volunteers express an interest in taking part in your study, you will be responsible for following up any interested volunteers and managing their recruitment.
