What does the study involve?

If you choose to participate, and are eligible, the study team will deliver the test kit to your address, which contains different taste strips. You will then be asked to join an online (Teams) meeting to answer the questionnaire. The questionnaire may take between 60-90 minutes.

Location(s)

Online (from your own home, or anywhere with internet access).

Expenses or incentives

If you take part in the full study you will be offered a Â£20 Amazon voucher.

Other details

This study is being run with the support of the NHS and the National Institute for Health and Care Research, working in partnership with the University of Nottingham and funded by Diabetes UK.

As a Be Part of Research volunteer, you may have received an invitation to join this or another study before. If you are currently taking part in another study, please check with the study team before indicating your interest in this, or other studies.
