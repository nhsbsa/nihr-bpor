Check if you can be part of this research

We need to check it’s safe and appropriate for you to take part in this study.

To do this, we’ll ask you a short set of questions. After you’ve answered these, we’ll let you know straight away if you can continue to the next stage of the study.

We might ask about:

  • health conditions you have
  • medication you take
{% call details({ summaryText: "How we handle your data" }) %}

If you can take part in this study, we:

  • only store that you’re eligible to take part - we’ll delete this information once it’s no longer needed for this study
  • do not store any of the data linked to the health conditions or medications that make you eligible

If you cannot take part, we do not keep any of your data relating to this study.

This information only applies to the data you give us to check if you can take part in this particular study. General data linked to your Be Part of Research account is not affected.
